# `EngineLab` 🚘
Have you ever imagined modifying the specifications of a car engine, then testing its performance exactly how you want? Well, `EngineLab` lets you do that!

`EngineLab`is a small website where you can test/simulate the performance of a engine of a vehicle, modifing its specifications.

---

## Features

- Modify engine specifications, **exactly how you want**
- Modify specifications like **engine size, maximum RPM, turbo and more**
- **Accelerate and push the engine to the limits**, just the way you want
- Choose the **weight, type of brakes and aerodynamics rating** of the car
